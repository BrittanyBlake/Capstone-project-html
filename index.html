How it works
buscar academias works in four simple steps:
1-search:

It's simple. On buscar academia's homepage, enter the name of a school you are looking for and click on search. alternatively, click on the search link on the top menu to view the list of all schools.

2-Locate:

get to see where hte school is located by clicking on the school profile. it's even more exciting. you can get the directions from anywhere in the country

3-Apply:

apply for your child's interview to the school of your choice. click on the APPLY button to get the application form. we also follow up on the application to make sure you get a reply on time.

4-Relax:

once your child gets admitted, you can now relax. you can check out our blog to see what we recommend when school hunting.
